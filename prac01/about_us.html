The Northern Australian Conference (NAC) is a conference of the Seventh Day Adventist Church in North queensland. The youth, known as Adventist Youth Aitkenvale or AYA consists of young adults ages 18-25 and pathfinders ages 13-18. Both groups are located in Aitkenvale, Townsville.
